## **🌐 Protocol, Not Just a Platform**

While the Doecial Sphere is a standalone implementation, its underlying **Bauta-Keating logic** is designed as a modular **Digital Public Infrastructure (DPI)** protocol.

* **Universal Portability:** The Bauta-Keating components can be exported to any digital public park or municipal deliberation space to provide an immediate "Trust Layer."  
* **Standardized Neutrality:** This enables diverse communities to implement "Deliberative Neutrality" by default, regardless of their legacy tech stack, by utilizing our established model adaptation and guardianship repos.
