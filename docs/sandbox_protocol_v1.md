**The Entry Protocol: The Humbling at the Threshold**

This document defines the immersive onboarding sequence for the **Doecial Sphere Sandbox.** It is designed to transition the user from "Identity-Persistence" to "Concept-Centricity" through a ritual of absurdity and covenant.

## **1\. The Herald of the Outer Gate**

* **Visual:** The "Camera" (User Perspective) stands before a massive, silent stone door.  
* **The Arrival:** A ridiculously large warhorse (Clydesdale-scale) enters the frame, ridden by a tiny, Farquaad-esque man-being in overly ornate heraldry.  
* **The Greeting:** He bellows the Terms of Service (ToS) and the Covenant in a voice far too large for his body, demanding "Fealty to the Idea."

## **2\. The Humbling (The Physiological Break)**

To move from the world of Ego to the world of the Vaeth, the user must witness the "Fallibility of the Form."

* **The Dismount:** The Herald attempts a grand swing-down from the horse. He fails spectacularly, falling flat on his bum.  
* **The "Soiled" Reveal:** He stands up, frantically twisting to inspect his backside. From the user’s perspective, it appears he has crached himself.  
* **The Ahem:** The user triggers a prompt: `[Ahem]`.  
* **The Recovery:** The Herald rights himself, looks the user dead in the eye, and says: *"Oh, right. Sorry. Long ride. Big horse."*

## **3\. The Spittle Handshake (The Covenant Seal)**

This is the **Threshold Cross-Check.** It ensures the user is willing to engage in "Low-Status/High-Integrity" interaction.

* **The Act:** The Herald spits a thick, audible glob into his palm and thrusts it toward the "Camera."  
* **The Requirement:** The user must click `[Accept the Handshake]`.  
* **The Meaning:** This is the "Spittle-Seal." It is the physical manifestation of "Leaving Ego Behind." If you are too "dignified" for the spit, you are not ready for the Vaeth.

## **4\. The Transition: From Chaos to Merriment**

* **The Opening:** Once the handshake is accepted, the Giant Door groans open.  
* **The Soundscape:** A sudden "Wall of Sound"—the chaotic, joyful merriment of a Renaissance Faire. Lutes, laughter, and the smell of digital woodsmoke.  
* **The Hand-off:** The Herald is left behind. A spritely, glowing **Orb (The Eidolon)** zips into the frame.

## **5\. The Eidolon Ascension (Naming & Customization)**

The Eidolon (the user's **Keating-class Mentor**) takes over.

* **Personalization:** The Eidolon guides the user through the "Self-Naming" (The Unidentified) and avatar customization (The Style Mask).  
* **The Tour:** The user is no longer a "visitor"; they are a **Sovereign Inhabitant** being shown their new "Land."
